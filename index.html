Saki Market Game
A Novel Fair Market Framework

Saki Market Game (SMG) is a novel decentralized AI-driven market framework that combines game theory, adaptive pricing, blockchain settlement, anti-collusion mechanisms, Proof of Contribution Competition (PoCC), and fairness constraints to create transparent and efficient markets for energy, oil, EV charging, and other strategic sectors.

⚙️ Adaptive AI pricing competition
🔗 Blockchain-based settlement
⚖️ Fairness and regulatory guardrails
🛡️ Anti-collusion and moderator logic

Introduction

Saki Market Game is designed as a new market-design framework in which buyer and seller agents do not simply follow static equations. Instead, they compete, adapt, respond to constraints, and converge toward a fairer market outcome under transparent rules.

SMG brings together economic competition, AI learning, blockchain-based settlement, and fairness-oriented governance in one integrated system.

AI Adaptive multi-agent pricing and market response
Fairness Guardrails, contribution, and anti-collusion logic
Blockchain Transparent settlement and recorded market outcomes

Its Novelty

The novelty of Saki Market Game lies in the fact that it is not merely a pricing model, not only a blockchain, and not only an AI marketplace. It combines several disciplines into one unified mechanism: adaptive game-theoretic pricing, multi-agent competition, contribution-based settlement, anti-collusion design, fairness constraints, and real-world executable market allocation.

Instead of treating equilibrium as a static mathematical endpoint, Saki Market Game treats it as a dynamic competitive learning process.

DDFM

Democratic Decentralized Fair Market is the market design concept introduced through SMG, where fairness, transparency, and decentralized competition are built into the rules.

PoCC

Proof of Contribution Competition links market participation, efficiency, and contribution to incentives and settlement, rather than relying only on conventional mining or centralized scoring.

Saki Energy

Saki Energy represents the application of SMG principles to energy corridors, oil trade, EV charging, and future strategic resource markets where fair access and adaptive allocation matter.

Why SMG Is Different

  • It combines AI competition with game-theoretic market behavior.
  • It integrates blockchain settlement directly into the market design.
  • It includes anti-collusion monitoring and moderator intervention.
  • It respects capacity, utility, price, quality, and fairness constraints together.
  • It converts continuous optimization into discrete real-world execution.
