Saki Market Game
A Novel Fair Market Framework

Saki Market Game (SMG) is a novel decentralized AI-driven market framework that combines game theory, adaptive pricing, blockchain settlement, anti-collusion mechanisms, Proof of Contribution Competition (PoCC), and fairness constraints.

⚙️ Adaptive AI pricing competition
🔗 Blockchain-based settlement
⚖️ Fairness and regulatory guardrails
🛡️ Anti-collusion and moderator logic

Introduction

Saki Market Game is designed as a new market-design framework where buyer and seller agents compete, adapt, and converge toward a fairer market outcome under transparent rules.

Its Novelty

Saki Market Game is not only a pricing model, not only a blockchain, and not only an AI marketplace. It combines adaptive game-theoretic pricing, multi-agent competition, contribution-based settlement, anti-collusion design, and fairness constraints into one unified framework.

DDFM

Democratic Decentralized Fair Market is the core market design concept introduced through SMG.

PoCC

Proof of Contribution Competition links market participation and contribution to settlement and rewards.

Saki Energy

SMG principles can be applied to oil, EV charging, and global energy corridors.
